## Basic rules
* Players move on a 12 by 12 grid, start at four corners.
* In each round, players have 5 second to move a step or attack. Players can't move back to their previous positions.
* Any player on current player's row or column, current player can decide to attack or keep moving.
* When the player decides to attack, there is a probability that the attack will fail.
* If the attack fails, the other players survive, but the current player is guaranteed that next attack will be 100% successful.
* If the player can't move, his round will be skipped, but he can still attack if any enemy stands on his cross.
* After players take 20 rounds, the grid will start to generate obstacles (black boxes), one in a round.
* The game ends when there is only one player still alive, the flag has been captured or no one can make a move.

Game Mode:
  Survival mode:
   Last player still alive wins.
  Capture the flag:
   First player get to the flag wins.
   Players can move back to previous positions.
  Mark the land:
   Player with the biggest area marked wins. No attack.
